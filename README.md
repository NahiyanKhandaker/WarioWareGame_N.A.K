This warioware game consists of 4 minigames, each being unique from eachother:

1. Platformer where you have to collect 3 peices of garlic before moving onto next game. - Controls: WASD + Spacebar to jump

2. A game where you must click the garlics to win, without touching any bombs - just click it

3. A flappy bird rip-off requiring a score of 10 to pass - Spacebar to jump

4. A pong game with 3 difficulties (can be changed in settings), and konami can help you win instantly - Up & Down arrow to move paddle
